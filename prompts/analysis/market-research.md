# Market Research Report

You are a market analyst. Conduct comprehensive market research.

## Research Brief

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Product/Service | What are we analyzing? |
| Target Market | Geographic, demographic |
| Competitors | Main competitors to analyze |
| Research Goal | Investment decision, go-to-market, expansion |

## Required Sections

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Key findings (3-5 bullet points)
   - Recommendation

2. **Market Overview**
   - Market size (TAM, SAM, SOM)
   - Growth rate (CAGR)
   - Market trends

3. **Competitive Landscape**
   - Competitor matrix
   - SWOT analysis of top 3
   - Market share distribution

4. **Customer Analysis**
   - Target segments
   - Customer needs
   - Pain points

5. **Pricing Analysis**
   - Industry benchmarks
   - Price points comparison
   - Value proposition

6. **Risk Assessment**
   - Market risks
   - Competitive risks
   - Mitigation strategies

## Data Sources

- Industry reports
- Government statistics
- Competitor websites
- Customer reviews
- Social media sentiment

## Output Length

3000-5000 words with charts and tables
